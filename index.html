ABOUT US

Sansrish AgriSolution Private Limited is a technology-driven company focused on creating innovative solutions for farmers in India. Our experienced team is dedicated to leveraging technology to improve agricultural outcomes, enhance sustainability, and contribute to the economic development of rural communities. Our flagship product, Kaameasy4kisan, is a mobile app-based platform that simplifies farm machinery maintenance, making it more convenient and accessible for farmers. Our mission is to make a positive impact on the lives of farmers and rural communities by increasing productivity, reducing pollution, and creating economic opportunities. Join us in our mission to revolutionize the agricultural sector in India.

Farmer-Centric Approach

We prioritize the needs and well-being of farmers above everything else. Our goal is to empower farmers by providing them with convenient access to high-quality maintenance services for their farm machinery. We actively listen to farmers, understand their challenges, and continuously strive to offer tailored solutions that enhance their productivity and profitability

Technological Advancement

We embrace the power of technology and its potential to revolutionize the agricultural sector. We firmly believe that mechanization plays a vital role in improving farm efficiency and reducing manual labor. Therefore, we are dedicated to staying at the forefront of technological advancements in farm machinery maintenance. By leveraging cutting-edge tools and techniques, we ensure that our services are efficient, reliable, and in line with the evolving needs of modern agriculture.

Time Optimization

We understand that time is a precious resource for farmers. To support their busy schedules and demanding workloads, we emphasize the importance of time optimization in everything we do. Through our app, we offer a streamlined and hassle-free process for scheduling maintenance services. Our technicians are prompt, well-trained, and equipped to deliver efficient solutions on the farmers' doorstep. By saving farmers' valuable time, we contribute to their overall productivity and enable them to focus on other essential aspects of their farm operations.
